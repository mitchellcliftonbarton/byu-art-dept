How to apply

The Department of Art consists of the following undergraduate programs: BA in ART, BFA in ART, and BA in Art Education. The Disciplines in the department include: Drawing, Painting, Printmaking, New Genre, Ceramics & Sculpture, Art Education and Art Education minor. The Art Department accepts applications for incoming students twice a year, March 30th and November 30th. Online submissions will begin 1 month before the deadline.

Admission to the Art Department occurs following the completion of freshman art core classes for your desired area (please see your MAP) and requires a portfolio submission.
